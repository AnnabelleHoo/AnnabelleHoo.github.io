What is Deisgn Thinking?

Design Thinking is the bridge between creativity and innovation, combining divergent and convergent modes of problem discovery and problem solving that leverages human insights and data analytics to create values. Throughout the process, user-experience, tech-nology tools/platforms, and business models will be integrated for fostering in-novation.
